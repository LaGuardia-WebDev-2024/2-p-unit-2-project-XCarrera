Culture

Singapore has a combination of Asian (Malay / Tamil / Chinese) and European cultures, mainly by Malay, South Asian, East Asian and Eurasian influences.
This blend of cultures makes for some very delicious cuisine. Some Singaporean dishes are kaya toast, chilli crab, fish head curry, laksa, roti prata and Hainanese chicken rice.
Singapore also has a lot of celebrations and festivals some of these include but are not limited to Pongal, Chinese New Year and Thaipusam.

The distance from NYC to Singapore is 9537 miles / 15349 kilometers this makes the travel time around 18 hours and 40 minutes if on a nonstop flight.
